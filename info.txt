A productivity app by Roman Zharkov.
Set the frequency of the alarm
and the duration of your task.
Skip the current phase by
clicking the Skip button.
Press Esc to exit.

You can set the window to always
be on top of the other windows
and set the opacity of the window
using the opacity slider.
